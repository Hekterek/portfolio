Me and the code

Hello!

I'm Karol, a person who has been drawn into the world of IT and who doesn't want to leave it.

For some time now, I've found what I was looking for in the field of computer science - the ability to create new things and to be free. And programming is precisely what gives me that. I started my journey with the front end and without hesitation, I quickly got to know the fundamental principles of coding in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Writing code on this side gives me the opportunity to beautifully present content to future users in a clean, clear, and very intuitive way.

But knowing only the front end, I had a feeling as if I were living with just one hand. Then it was time for the back end. Since Java fell into my hands, I felt like a omnipotent person. From that moment on, I can gradually and precisely create whatever I want. With such possibilities, I immediately started writing my project that will help many people deal with managing their finances. More about that in another tab !

The only problem with programming is that the more we delve into different technologies, the hungrier we become for new ways and possibilities that these technologies offer us.

It's not a problem, it's a side effect !
